About Us


Correctly separating own waste brings benefits to the environment, polluting it less, to the community by making the roads cleaner and the health of the individual citizen. The positive effects are also of an economic nature. First of all, the economic savings on disposal costs, greater efficiency during collection operations, greater use of recycled materials.

Every citizen, with conscience, is required to put a limit on the waste of resources and pollution by sorting waste properly and wasting less. Buying products on tap, preferring glass to plastic so that you can reuse the packaging are virtuous attitudes that, if combined with the habit of recycling, can produce a visible change and partially reverse the destiny of a planet that suffers due to raising of temperatures and climate change. In the municipalities, special bins with colors that vary according to the location (yellow, green, white, brown, red and even fuchsia), allow the correct disposal of waste.
